CupCore is a modification for the classic run and gun action game known as Cuphead. In this mod players will be able to take on even more challenging bosses whilst experiencing unused and new content never seen before!

Originally started by GabenZone in late March 2018 the project was redone by Grub4K (Simon Sawicki) and nicman (Nicolas Hawrysh) with the help of ayy lmao (Chris Ugalde) 155sparkles (Angus Wells), GabenZone, Filthy, Dashaque, EpicalaxyMaster, NikoWazHere and Apok.
